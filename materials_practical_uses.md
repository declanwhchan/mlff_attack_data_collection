## Source Quality Notes

- Rows marked `high` generally have exact-compound evidence and a direct device, catalyst, industrial, or engineering context.
- Rows marked `medium` are still useful applied-literature matches, but the practical use is less commercialized, relies on doped/modified variants, or is mostly computational/proposed.
- `beta_cristobalite_sio2` and `beta_quartz_sio2` are phase-specific entries in the dataset; their practical mapping is mostly through silica/quartz phase behavior and quartz-device literature rather than a room-temperature beta-phase product.
